# Software Engineer, Infrastructure

## About the Role

Amadeus Airline Solutions team is looking for a Software Engineer to design and develop a high‑performance Business Rule Engine built on a distributed, event‑driven microservices architecture within the airline domain. The platform processes real‑time streaming events—including PNRs, tickets, schedules, and inventory updates—at scale, and applies configurable business rules to automate complex operational decisions across airline systems.

## Responsibilities

- Design and build fault‑tolerant microservices using Kafka‑based pipelines
- Optimize large‑scale, low‑latency event processing to ensure reliable automation under high traffic volumes
- Improve system resilience through DLQ handling, replay mechanisms, on‑call observability, and production‑grade troubleshooting
- Enhance scalability of rule evaluation and action orchestration across multi‑stack airline environments (Etihad, Air Canada, Finnair, Saudia, etc.)
- Collaborate across engineering, ETL, and DevOps teams to maintain system stability and evolve the rule processing ecosystem
- Troubleshoot and resolve production incidents in mission‑critical airline workflows

## Minimum Qualifications

- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, related technical field, or equivalent practical experience
- 3+ years of professional software development experience
- Strong experience with Java (Java 8+) in production environments
- Experience with distributed systems and event‑driven architectures
- Experience with message brokers and stream processing (Kafka or equivalent)

## Preferred Qualifications

- Experience in the airline or travel domain
- Experience with microservices architecture and containerization
- Experience building and maintaining large‑scale data processing pipelines
- Knowledge of ETL processes and data integration patterns
- Experience with observability tools (monitoring, logging, tracing)
- Strong problem‑solving skills and ability to work in mission‑critical environments
- Commitment to reliability, correctness, and operational excellence

## Our Stack

- **Languages:** Java 8

- **Domain:** Airline systems (PNRs, tickets, schedules, inventory)
